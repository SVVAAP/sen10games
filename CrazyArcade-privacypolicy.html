Privacy Policy for CrazyArcade

Effective date: April 22, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Sen10Games ("we", "us", or "our") handles information when you use the CrazyArcade mobile application (the "App").

1. Information We Collect

Based on the current version of the App, we do not directly collect personal information such as your name, email address, or phone number.

The App currently uses local gameplay data (for example, score, streak, and game progress) to run game features on your device.

2. Ads, Leaderboards, and Backend Services

The current codebase includes placeholder integrations for rewarded ads, leaderboard submission, and backend services. These services may be enabled in future releases.

If enabled in a future update, third-party providers may collect device or usage information as required for their services (for example ad delivery, fraud prevention, or leaderboard functionality). We will update this Privacy Policy before or when those features are activated.

3. How We Use Information

  • To provide and maintain gameplay features.
  • To improve game quality, balance, and performance.
  • To support optional features such as ads or leaderboards if enabled.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. If third-party services are enabled in future versions, only the minimum information needed to provide those features will be shared with those service providers.

5. Data Retention

Local game data is stored on your device and remains until you clear app data or uninstall the App.

6. Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact us so we can address the issue.

7. Security

We take reasonable steps to protect information related to the App. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

8. Your Choices

  • You can stop all data processing by uninstalling the App.
  • If optional online services are added later, additional controls may be provided in-app.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the updated version with a new effective date.
